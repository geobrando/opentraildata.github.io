OpenTrails at the Summit!

Code for America Summit is next week, and it’s going to be awesome. OpenTrails will be making several appearances at the Summit, and we want to make sure you know about them. We can’t wait to see everyone and share the latest news and ideas for OpenTrails!

Tuesday, September 23rd

Trailhead Labs at the Civic Tech Fair

6-8pm @ Yerba Buena Center Forum

Come meet the team behind Trailhead Labs, the first commercial implementation of the OpenTrails standard. We’re OpenTrails Course technical facilitators and generally nice folks to chat with!

Wednesday, September 24th

Publishing Data in OpenTrails Workshop

9-9:45am @ Intercontinental Hotel

OpenTrails rockstars Lyzi Diamond and Alan Williams, with help from Jereme Monteau and Ryan Branciforte, will introduce and lead you through the process of adopting and making OpenTrails work for your citizens!

Thursday, September 25th

Trailhead Labs at the Accelerator Roundtable

11-11:45AM @ Yerba Buena Center

Come hear Ryan Branciforte, CEO of Trailhead Labs, tell the story of making open data work for trails, the challenges involved and the solutions we are working on.

Defaulting to Open

2-2:45 @ Yerba Buena Center

Code for America Fellowship alum Alan Williams and Trailhead Labs CEO Ryan Branciforte will expound on the value of parks, good design and the user experience that is enabled with open data as a foundation.

Meet the OpenTrails Community

3:30-4: @ Yerba Buena Garden (Directly in front of YBCA)

Grab a coffee and drink it outside while meeting and greeting all of the fantastic folks that are making OpenTrails a reality. After, head back inside to check out one of our favorite projects, Transitmix!
